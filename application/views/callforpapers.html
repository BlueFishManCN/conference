2018 International Conference on Transformations and Innovations in Management
September 9-10, 2017, Shanghai, China.

SHU-UTS SILC Business School, Shanghai University and UTS Business School, University of Technology, Sydney, would like to invite you to participate the International Conference on Transformations and Innovations in Management (ICTIM), September 9-10, 2017, in Shanghai China. The conference is jointly organized by Shanghai University in China and University of Technology, Sydney in Australia. All accepted full papers will be published by Atlantis Press and be indexed by CPCI-SSH (ISTP). Selected papers will be published in a special issue of the journal Transformations in Business & Economics (SSCI-indexed journal) in March 2018 (additional publication fees apply). We will also recommend other high quality papers to several international journals for consideration for publication. All papers should be written in English.

The central theme is Transformations and Innovations in Management, and all papers, both theoretical and empirical, are welcome. The covered fields may include but not limited to:

Innovation and Entrepreneurship
Organizational Behavior
Strategic Management
International Management
Human Resource Management
Marketing Management
Engineering Management
Financial Management
Management issues in Economics, Finance, Banking, and Accounting
Logistics and Supply Chain Management
Sustainability and Sustainable Development
Information Technology and Management
Educational Management
Tourism Management
Agriculture and Forestry Management
Public Administration

The official conference language is English. Best conference papers and best student papers will be selected and awarded at the conference. Authors should submit their papers by e-mail to the following e-mail address: ictim2017@126.com. If you have any questions please contact Dr.Derek Zheng of Shanghai University, Email: ictim2017@126.com. Tel: +86 17717013186.
