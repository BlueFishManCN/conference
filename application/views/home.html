Welcome
International Conference on Global Economics and Governance is initiatedand hosted by the Institute for Economic Forecasting and co-hosted by the National University of Political Studies and Public Administration. The conference aimsto provide a forum for accessing to the latest theoretical and empirical knowledge from both modern economy and finance. The goal of this conference isto bring researchers, practitioners and officials to the areas of globale conomics and governances to share experiences and original research contributions to those topics. Since 2013, GEG have been organized in Wuhan University, Romania, Ming Chuan University, Ocean University of China, and Poland. In October 2018, the 6th GEG will be organized in Shanghai University.
